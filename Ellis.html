My Top 3 Favorite Video Games

1) Fortnite Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is a multiplayer shooter game where you start out inside a flying battle bus and drop intro many points of interest on and island. Players must scavenge for resources, collect weapons,and fight players. A storm pushes players together so they can fight to be the last one standing.

2) Rainbow Six Seige

Rainbow Six Seige is a multiplayer shooter game where you are an operator that has to either defend of attack a position. You have to move stragicly to an objective to either defuse a bomb, extract a hostage or eliminate all of the opposing team.

3) Player Unknown's Battle Grounds (PUBG)

Player Unknown's Battle grounds or PUBG is a battle royale shooter game. You start out in a plane flying over an island and have to jump out to different points of interest. You need to look for supplies like weapons, armour, and med kits. A storm pushes all of the players together to fight to be the last one standing and to get the winner winner chicken dinner.
